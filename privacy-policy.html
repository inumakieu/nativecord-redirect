Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24.04.2025

This Privacy Policy describes how NativeCord ("we", "our", or "us") handles your data.

1. What Information We Collect

When you log in using Discord OAuth2, we may collect the following data based on the scopes you authorize:

  • Your Discord user ID, username, and avatar
  • Your email address (if authorized)
  • Your list of servers ("guilds")
  • Messages you send or receive within accessible channels
  • Your access token for authentication

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data to provide functionality within the NativeCord app. This includes:

  • Displaying your account information
  • Showing your servers and channels
  • Allowing you to read and send messages

Message content is used only for display and interaction within the app. We do not store, log, or transmit your messages to any server.

3. Data Storage and Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded or sent to third-party servers. All data is cleared when you log out or uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

NativeCord connects directly to the Discord API. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke access at any time from your Discord settings.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository.
